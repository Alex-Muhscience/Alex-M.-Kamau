My Journey

My fascination with technology began with dismantling electronics to understand their inner workings. This curiosity evolved into a passion for programming and cybersecurity during my high school years, leading me to pursue Computer Science at Kisii University.

At university, I immersed myself in Data Structures, Algorithms, Network Programming, and AI, while actively participating in tech competitions. My project on Ethical AI earned recognition in Mozilla's Responsible Computing Challenge, reinforcing my commitment to developing technology responsibly.

Professional experiences at Secunets Technologies Ltd and Gicatech Enterprise Solutions honed my skills in cybersecurity, network infrastructure, and software development. I've contributed to significant projects like the Sintel GSMA_SAS_UP Security Audit and developed practical solutions like the KeyGen Password Manager.

What excites me most about technology is its potential to solve complex problems while maintaining ethical standards. My work on the Unified Threat Detection and Response System (UTDRS) combines AI with cybersecurity to protect SMEs, demonstrating this philosophy in action.

Innovator's Mindset

I believe the best solutions come from interdisciplinary thinking. My projects often blend cybersecurity, AI, and blockchain to create novel approaches to digital challenges.

Beyond technical work, I'm committed to knowledge sharing through open-source contributions and tech community engagement. I'm particularly interested in making cybersecurity accessible to African SMEs and exploring decentralized solutions for data privacy.
